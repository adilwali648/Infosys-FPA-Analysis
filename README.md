Infosys FY2025 — Budget vs Actual Variance Analysis

 A Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) project built on Infosys Limited's FY 2025 financials, comparing actual performance   against budget across Revenue, Expenses, and Profitability.

 Project Structure
 Raw Data - Historical financials for FY 2022, FY 2023, FY 2024 with 3-year CAGR 
 Budget Assumption - FY 2025 budget built using growth assumptions on FY 2024 actuals 
 Variance Analysis - Actual vs Budget comparison with Variance, Variance %, F/U flag, and YoY Growth 
 CFO Commentary - Management commentary summarising performance across all three sections 

 Key Highlights
- Revenue came in at ₹1,62,990 Cr — marginally below budget by 1.8% but up 6.1% YoY
- Total Expenses were ₹1,456 Cr below budget — reflecting strong cost discipline
- EBIT Margin held at 39.6% vs budgeted 39.9% — compression of just 20 bps
- Net Profit of ₹26,713 Cr at PAT margin of 16.4%, in line with historical range of 16–18%

 Skills Demonstrated
- Budget modelling using historical CAGR-based assumptions
- Variance analysis with F/U (Favourable/Unfavourable) classification
- YoY growth analysis across revenue, cost, and profitability lines
- CFO-style management commentary writing
- Excel formula construction — IFERROR, IF, SUM, cross-sheet references
  
 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Financial data sourced from Infosys Limited publicly available annual reports
